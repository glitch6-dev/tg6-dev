Privacy Policy

Plain English. I only collect what I need to reply to you and deliver what you paid for.

Last updated: 2026

What I collect

When you submit the application form, I collect the details you enter — your name, email, background, goal, the project you want to ship, your timeline, and why now. That's it. If you email me, I keep that thread.

Payments

Checkout is handled by Stripe. Card details go straight to Stripe — they never touch this site or any server I run. I only receive confirmation that a purchase happened and the email tied to it.

How I use it

  • To reply to your application and, if it's a fit, set up a call.
  • To give you access to the program you bought.
  • To occasionally email you about your enrollment. No spam, no list-selling.

Who else sees it

Application data lands in a private Google Sheet and an email to me. The processors involved are Google (Forms/Sheets/Gmail) and Stripe (payments). I don't sell or share your information with anyone else.

Cookies & tracking

This site sets no advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. Stripe may set cookies during checkout to process your payment securely.

Your choices

Want your data corrected or deleted? Email dvelupr@proton.me and I'll handle it.
